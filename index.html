Subscription Support

  • All subscriptions are managed through your Apple App Store account.
  • To cancel a subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions, and select Intervene.

App Usage Support

  • All your data is stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to external servers.
  • Your rewire history, triggers, and achievements are private and stay on your device.
  • If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted. Please back up any important information before uninstalling.
  • If the app is not launching or behaves unexpectedly, try restarting your device or reinstalling the app.

FAQ / Common Issues

  • App not opening: Ensure your iOS version is supported (iOS 17.6+) and fully updated.
  • Lost data: Data is only stored locally. If the app is uninstalled, data cannot be recovered.
  • Widget not showing: Long-press your home screen, tap the + button, search for "Intervene" and add the widget.
  • Subscription not working: Make sure you are logged in with the Apple ID used to purchase the subscription.
